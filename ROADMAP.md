# Roadmap

- Sprint 0: Complete — Windows foundation and read-only diagnostics (`v0.1.0-alpha.1`).
- Sprint 1: Complete and accepted — production-grade mesh diagnostics, evidence selection, bounded Deep checks, performance evidence, and schema 2.0 (`0.2.0-alpha.1`).
- Sprint 2: Complete and accepted by automated gates — protected workspace repair, evidence-bound plans, eight controlled operations, checkpoints, undo/restore, accept/rollback, comparison, and repair audit schema 1.0 (`0.3.0-alpha.1`). Installed-panel and real-statue UAT remain deferred.
- Sprint 3: Printability validation and print-oriented export.
- Sprint 4: Controlled remeshing.
- Sprint 5: Chroma3D internal UAT.
- Sprint 6: Reference and asset workflows.
- Later phases: bounded AI-assisted planning and authoring workflows.
- Later commercial work: packaging, licensing, support, and distribution design.

The roadmap does not promise fully autonomous museum-quality sculpting or guaranteed print success.
